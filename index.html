About Us

Born from firsthand experience as a student, grader, and researcher, RAISE is our response to the overwhelming amount of administrative work placed on educators today.

RAISE stands for Responsible AI for Student Engagement. We're building an AI-powered teaching assistant platform designed to help professors and educators streamline their most time-consuming tasks—so they can focus on what really matters: teaching and inspiring students.

We believe AI can do more than automate—it can enhance learning experiences and humanize education at scale.

The Problem

Professors are under intense pressure with administrative tasks that take away from meaningful teaching time.

Overwhelming Admin Work

Professors are expected to grade hundreds of assignments, answer repetitive student questions, and track student performance across huge classes.

Time Constraints

All of this administrative burden adds up to less time for meaningful teaching and providing personalized student support.

Student Engagement Challenges

Large class sizes make it difficult to identify struggling students and address individual learning needs effectively.

Our Solution

RAISE is a centralized AI platform that acts as a 24/7 teaching assistant, handling repetitive tasks and providing valuable insights.

AI Quiz & Assignment Generator

Generate Bloom's Taxonomy-based questions from lecture slides, textbooks, or syllabi, saving hours of preparation time.

Auto-Grader with Feedback

Automatically grade code, short answers, or essays with structured AI-generated feedback that helps students learn from their mistakes.

Course-Specific Chatbot (AI Office Hours)

Trained on course content, this feature answers repetitive student queries anytime—"What's due this week?", "Explain Merge Sort."

Real-Time Student Engagement Dashboard

Shows who's struggling, where learning gaps exist, and suggests interventions to improve student outcomes.

Adaptive Content Engine (Coming Soon)

Simplifies difficult topics, creates summaries, and recommends additional resources based on individual learning levels.

Tech Stack (MVP)

Built with modern, scalable technologies to ensure performance, security, and ease of integration.

Component Stack
LLM Backend OpenAI GPT-4 / Claude / Mistral
Retrieval (RAG) LangChain + Chroma/Weaviate
Frontend React.js + Tailwind CSS
LMS Integration LTI + Moodle / Canvas APIs
Backend FastAPI + PostgreSQL
Auto-Grading (Coding) Python + nbgrader/custom test suite
Hosting (MVP) GitHub Pages + Netlify/Vercel
